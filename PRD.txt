1. Product Overview
1.1 Product Name (Working Title)

(Brand-neutral internally; marketing name can change.)

Small businesses struggle with:

Too many disconnected tools (CRM, email, booking, website, forms)

Technical complexity

No clear setup or onboarding

They want one simple system, done for them, at a price they can afford.

A white-labeled, all-in-one platform that includes:
